#   AMAZING   ANIMES   TO   EXLORE   #
• One Piece is one of the longest-running manga-turned-anime series ever. Originally airing in 1999, One Piece is still a fan-favorite over two decades later. This action-based series is known for its terrific comedy,likable characters,often random occurrences,and interesting plots
• Based on the cultural award-winning manga of the same name, Kingdom's anime adaptation remains a huge hit with fans as it enters its third season.This anime is a great balance of strategy and action and has been praised for its storytelling.
• Jujutsu Kaisen became very popular in a short matter of time. Airing in October of 2020, this anime resonated with viewers and instantly shot up the most popular list, where it remains near the top. The anime follows protagonist Itadori Yuji, who winds up tangled in a complicated mess when he encounters a cursed item.
• Currently airing as of January 2021, Mushoku Tensei is an isekai anime with an interesting twist. After a life of bullying and harassment, the protagonist is killed in a tragic accident only to be reborn with his memories intact. However, he is born again in another world as a boy named Rudeus.
• Slice-of-life series often provide a nice break from fast-paced fantasy and action series, and Non Non Biyori Nonstop fits perfectly into this mold. A sequel to Non Non Biyori Repeat, this anime focuses on a new family including highschooler Akane moving into town and the adventures that happen in the countryside with the girls' newest friend.
• Holo Graffiti features the VTubers of Holo Live as they engage in all sorts of shenanigans. With the eccentric cast of characters, daily life is never easy, and there is always something going on that will have fans rolling in the aisles. From trying to fly to selling ramen on a deserted island, Holo Graffiti is sure to put a smile on every fan's face.
• Horimiya is a comedic slice-of-life anime centered around Hori and Miyamura, two classmates who are different people in school than they are outside of it. When Hori gets home, she becomes the mother of her household, trading her uniform for casual clothing and pinning her carefully styled hair up in whatever way is quickest so that she can take on the household chores, make dinner, and take care of her younger brother.
• Attack on Titan became a huge hit when the manga began publication in 2009 and gained an even larger fanbase when the anime began airing in 2013. Featuring a bleak world where humanity is at the mercy of creatures called Titans, fans cheered on underdog Eren and his Survey Corps comrades as they battled the Titans to free humanity.
